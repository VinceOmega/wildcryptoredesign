First, you’ll need to create an account. Do this by clicking the Sign Up button and filling in your details. It only takes a minute or two. Then deposit funds and you’re ready to get going. Click here to find out more.

To play Instant Win games, choose a game to play. You can either 'Try Game' for free or 'Play Game' for a chance to win. The rules are shown when you choose a game.

For AfroMillionsLotto, pick a line of six numbers between 1 and 49 as well as a Bonus Number between 0 and 9; or, to make life simple, just click Lucky Dip and your numbers will be chosen automatically. You can save your numbers to use again and you can add as many additional lines as you like, using a different selection of numbers for each.

When you’re happy with your choices, click Buy and you’re in the draw. Good luck!

Draws and Prizes

You can view your account details by clicking on My Account, including any future draws you’ve entered.

Draws are based on the German National Lottery and take place twice weekly, at 6.25pm Central European Time on Wednesday and 7.25pm Central European Time on Saturday. You can buy tickets up to half an hour before each draw and watch the draw live on German television at http://bit.ly/1uUtwoS. Results will appear on the homepage after the draw and on the Results page. Thirty minutes after the draw, tickets can be purchased for the next draw.

Don’t worry though, if you win a prize - small or large - we’ll let you know by sending you an email asking you to log-in to your account.

Our prize structure is simple. The numbers you have chosen in a single line must match the winning lottery numbers in order to win.

The jackpot starts at €2,500,000 and increases by €250,000 each draw (if it’s not won) until it reaches a maximum of €5,000,000. In the event that the jackpot is won, it reverts to €2,500,000.

If more than one person has matched the winning line, the jackpot is shared equally amongst those winners.
