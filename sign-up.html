F.A.Q

What is AfroMillionsLotto?

AfroMillionsLotto is a website where you can play games of chance. You can enter lotto draws by purchasing an online ticket to bet on the outcome of the German National Lottery, or play Instant Win games on screen to win cash prizes.

What is lotto?

Lotto is a game of chance. To play, you need to select 6 numbers from 1 to 49 and a Bonus Number from 0 to 9. Alternatively you can select numbers randomly using Lucky Dip. The lines are then entered into a prize draw where winning numbers are selected at random with prizes for selecting 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 numbers and a jackpot for picking 6 plus the Bonus Number. To learn more, go to 'How To Play' at the footer of this page. 

 

How do I enter a lotto draw?

You must set up an account, deposit funds and then make your selections before purchasing an online ticket. You can set up your account by clicking on 'Sign Up' on the homepage and follow the steps to 'Add Funds'.

How do I play Instant Win games?

You must set up an account and then deposit funds to play Instant Win games. You can set up your account by clicking on 'Sign Up' on the homepage and follow the steps to 'Add Funds'. Once you've deposited funds, choose the Instant Win game you would like to play and follow the simple instructions. Games you have purchased but not played will need to be played before you can play the exact same game again. You do not need to finish playing a game if you choose to play a different game.

How do I create an account?

You can set up your account by clicking on 'Sign Up' on the homepage and following the simple registration process. You will need to provide some personal information and confirm that you are aged 18 or over before adding funds to your account.  

How can I deposit funds into my account?

You can deposit funds through a variety of payment methods including credit card, debit card or e-wallet. To learn, more go to 'How to Add Funds' at the footer of this page. Please contact us if you have any difficulties.
